Project 3: California Housing

Business Goal

We are a real estate brokerage firm that is looking to predict future home sale prices based on publicly available data. We are most interested in the final price the home sold for based on specific variables that may have affected it. We want to use this data to improve our chances of getting the highest sale price possible for the area from potential buyers.

Our dependent variable is price.

Our independent variables are:

  • Latitude
  • Longitude
  • Zip Population
  • Zip Median Income
  • Number of Bedrooms
  • Number of Bathrooms
  • Square Feet
  • Lot Size
  • Age of the house in years
  • Days on Market
  • HOA Month
